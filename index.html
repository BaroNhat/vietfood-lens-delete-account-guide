Vietfood Lens Account Deletion & Data Management Guide

Deleting Your Entire Account

You can request to delete your entire Vietfood Lens account and all associated data directly from within the app. Please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Vietfood Lens app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile section (bottom navigation bar).
  3. On the Profile screen, tap on "My account" or a similar option to access your personal information editing page (Edit Profile).
  4. On the "Edit Profile" page, scroll down and find the "Delete Account" button.
  5. Carefully read the warning information in the confirmation dialog. You may be asked to confirm via an OTP sent to your registered email.
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the account deletion process.

When you delete your entire account:

  • Your account information (username, full name, email, profile picture if any) will be deleted.
  • Your entire food recognition history will be deleted.
  • All photos you uploaded for recognition or history purposes will be deleted.
  • Your favorited dishes and restaurants will be removed.
  • Fully anonymized data used for AI model improvement (if you previously consented) may not be deleted but will no longer be linked to your account.

Managing and Deleting Partial Data

In addition to deleting your entire account, Vietfood Lens allows you to manage and delete specific types of your data directly from within the app without deleting your account:

1. Deleting Individual Items from Recognition History

You can delete individual entries from your food recognition history:

  • Navigate to the "History" section.
  • Tap the trash can icon üóëÔ∏è next to that item.
  • A confirmation dialog will appear, tap the "Remove" button to complete.

2. Removing Dishes/Restaurants from Favorites

To remove a saved dish or restaurant from your favorites list:

  • Navigate to the "Favorites" section.
  • Find the dish or restaurant you want to remove.
  • Tap the trash can icon üóëÔ∏è next to that item.
  • A confirmation dialog will appear, tap the "Remove" button to complete.

The actions above will remove the corresponding data from your account. This does not affect other data or your Vietfood Lens account.

If you have any questions or encounter difficulties while managing or deleting your data/account, please contact us via email: adminnhat@unime.site.
